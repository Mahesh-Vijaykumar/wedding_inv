## Tips for Best Results

### For Midjourney:
1. Use `--ar 16:9` for proper aspect ratio
2. Use `--style expressive` for artistic look
3. Use `--quality 2` for highest quality
4. Use `--stylize 750` for more artistic interpretation
5. Try variations with `/imagine` command

### For DALL-E 3:
1. Be very descriptive
2. Mention "Ghibli" or "Studio Ghibli" explicitly
3. Request "no background" clearly
4. Ask for "4K quality" and "ultra detailed"
5. Can generate up to 4 variations

### For All AI Tools:
1. **Be specific** about colors, positions, and style
2. **Mention "symmetrical"** for balanced composition
3. **Request "no background"** or "transparent background"
4. **Reference "Ghibli"** for the desired art style
5. **Use "4K" and "ultra detailed"** for quality
6. **Iterate**: Generate multiple times and pick the best

## Post-Processing Steps

After generating your image:

1. **Remove Background** (if not transparent):
   - Use [Remove.bg](https://www.remove.bg/)
   - Or use Photoshop: Select > Subject > Inverse > Delete
   - Or use GIMP (free): Fuzzy Select Tool

2. **Optimize File Size**:
   - Use [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com/)
   - Or use [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/)
   - Target: Under 2MB

3. **Verify Quality**:
   - Check resolution is at least 1920x1080
   - Ensure transparency works
   - Verify colors match theme

4. **Save Correctly**:
   - Format: PNG-24 with alpha channel
   - Filename: `elephants-hero.png`
   - Location: `public/images/hero/`
